The first Ferrari-badged car was the 1947 125 Sport, powered by a 1.5 L V12 engine. On March 12, Enzo Ferrari took the car out for its first test-drive on the open roads. Two examples debuted on 11 May 1947 at the Piacenza racing circuit, driven by Franco Cortese and Nino Farina.
